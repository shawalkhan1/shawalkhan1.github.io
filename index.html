Flagship build

Production LLM system · the engine behind SYNQ

LLM Twin — A Digital Mind That Writes Like You

What if you could clone your own writing voice — and ship it as a real product? LLM Twin is exactly that: an AI that ingests everything you've ever written, learns how you think, and answers as you would. It's also the production blueprint that powers SYNQ below.

Data Feature Training Inference Serving

It started as a question — can a language model become a faithful replica of a single human's mind? Building it end-to-end meant solving every hard problem in modern LLM engineering at once: messy real-world data, fine-tuning at the edge of consumer hardware, retrieval that stays factual, and inference fast enough to feel alive. Here's the journey, stage by stage. ↓ tap a stage

A multi-source ETL crawler pulls every digital trace — GitHub, Medium, personal sites — into a MongoDB data warehouse. A Change-Data-Capture (CDC) layer + a Bytewax streaming pipeline keep the twin's memory continuously fresh, so it never goes stale as you keep writing.

Raw text is cleaned, chunked, and embedded into a Qdrant vector database. The RAG layer is no toy: query expansion, self-querying metadata filters, hybrid search, and a cross-encoder reranker ensure the twin retrieves what's actually relevant before it ever speaks.

Llama 3.1 8B fine-tuned with QLoRA/LoRA (TRL + Unsloth) on an instruction dataset built from your own content, then aligned to your preferences with DPO/RLHF. Every run is tracked, versioned, and evaluated with LLM-as-a-judge + RAGAS in Comet ML — no guessing whether it got better.

Deployed as a real-time AWS SageMaker endpoint behind a FastAPI microservice with a LangGraph agentic RAG brain. Squeezed with quantization (GPTQ/AWQ/GGUF), speculative decoding, Flash Attention, KV-cache & continuous batching — benchmarked across vLLM / TGI / TensorRT-LLM to pick the fastest stack.

The whole system runs on the Feature/Training/Inference (FTI) pattern, orchestrated by ZenML into independently deployable microservices. Full CI/CD/CT via GitHub Actions, Opik observability, and data + concept drift detection that triggers automatic retraining.

SYNQ

“Before people meet, their minds already have.”

● Compatibility infrastructure — not a dating app, social network, or AI companion
SYNQ is an AI-powered social compatibility & relationship-orchestration platform. Every user trains a high-fidelity AI Clone of themselves; these autonomous agents meet, converse, and probe values across the network, so a compatibility-intelligence layer surfaces high-probability human connections before a single human word is exchanged — across dating, friendship, mentorship, hiring, and co-founder discovery.
SYNQorchestrator
Clones meeting clones · the orchestrator listening in real time

Picture this. It's 3 a.m. and you're asleep — but you are still working. Across the network, your AI Clone is wide awake: it just finished a deep conversation with three other people's Clones, debating values, testing humor, probing how each of you handles conflict. It quietly read two long articles you saved, finished a book you never had time for, and listened to a 90-minute podcast at 2× speed.

You wake up to a single calm briefing: “Here's what I learned last night. Here are two people you'd genuinely click with — and why. Here's a 4-line summary of that book. And I drafted answers to the interview you have Thursday.” That is SYNQ — a second self that lives the parts of your life you don't have time for.
